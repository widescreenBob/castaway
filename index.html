What is Castaway 2024?

Castaway w/ Luau Lads started as an idea to shine a tropical spotlight on North Florida. Successful tiki weekenders in South Florida & California paved the way for us to build on the culture in our hometown of Jacksonville. With two successful years under our belt, The Lads are stretching Year 3 across two full days of fun.

With events located around Downtown/San Marco, & a room block at The Hyatt Regency, The Lads will show you an amazing time with Live Music, cocktails & Food offerings throughout the weekend. Guest Bars Bar Tiki(Florida) & Remora Bar(Utah) will join us to serve up some delicious offerings.
